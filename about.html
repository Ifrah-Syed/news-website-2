About News Bee

Hello there, welcome to News Bee. I am glad you are here. Wonna know about this website? I promise I won't bore you.

I, Ifrah Syed, developed this website with a vision of having an access to a hassle free news reading experience for the users. I usually find news website and newspapers packed with information and with annoying formats that makes it difficult to navigate through the sections of my interest.

Can anyone relate to the struggle to find a better news coverage from different news sources. I had to open numerous search tabs to go through different news sources so that I can find the video related to that news(that only one or two sources publish).

Having your effortless scroll through your daily news in the morning is very much needed. We don't wonna add to that morning grumpiness. Duhh!!

How can it ease your daily news scroll


Your news is majorly divided into 4 sections:
Country, Categories, Sources and Languages.


If you can't find your country, category, news source or language in the lists, we deeply apologize; this website is still in progress. We are going to add more options in the near future until we cover the globe.

Country

You wonna know what's cookin' in your country? Choose from the list of 20+ countries from 'top news' option in navigation bar and get the top news from top sources of your country.

Categories

Whether you are a tech junkie or intrigued by business, we've got you covered. Scroll through your area of interest with option 'categories' in the navigation bar.

Sources

Find your ideal news coverage by navigating through 10+ news sources, from BBC to TechCrunch. Go to the 'sources' in the navigation bar.

Languages

Nothing beats the feeling of being at home. Get your news in your mother tongue by selecting it from the 'languages' in the navigation bar.
